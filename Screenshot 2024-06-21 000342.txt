The pie chart reveals the distribution of foreign direct investment (FDI) inflows into the healthcare sector in a specific region (cumulative data from April 2000 to March 2022). Drugs and pharmaceuticals attract the largest share of the FDI (66.41%), followed by hospitals and diagnostic centers (19.41%). Medical and surgical appliances account for a smaller portion (14.18%) of the total FDI. This data suggests that investors prioritize pharmaceutical companies and healthcare facilities over medical device manufacturers in this region.
