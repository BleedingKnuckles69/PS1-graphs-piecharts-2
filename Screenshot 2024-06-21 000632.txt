In 2019, the United States achieved a 41% share of recycled water in its total annual water withdrawal, demonstrating a remarkable accomplishment in water reuse. Despite this progress, freshwater sources are still heavily relied upon (59%). This pie chart underscores the importance of both continued investment in water recycling technologies and sustainable management of freshwater resources to meet the growing water demands of the country.
